A New Beginning

Care Club’s First Steps Toward Change; Just a few months after the birth of Care Club Charity Organization in 2022, the spirit of service has already begun to ripple across communities. What started as a small initiative driven by compassion has now taken its first real steps, feeding families, supporting children, and building trust in places where hope was fading. In January, we launched our first food and clothing distribution, reaching over 50 families living in difficult conditions. Volunteers came together, most of them youth, carrying not just bags of food but messages of encouragement, love, and solidarity. The smiles we saw that day reminded us why Care Club exists; to bring hope where there is struggle.

We also began identifying children in need of school support. With schools reopening, our goal was clear, no child should stay behind because of poverty. Already, we’ve managed to commit to supporting the education of a small group of learners, with the hope of expanding as more resources and partners come in. Our team may still be small, but the vision is bold. Care Club is not just a project, it’s a movement fueled by young hearts who believe in change, and by community members who see the power of coming together. We are learning, growing, and building something that will last. As of this first day of February 2023, we celebrate not the numbers, but the impact, the unity, and the faith that this journey is just beginning. We’re only getting started.

About us

The Care Club Charity is a Non profit and empowerment organization located in Plot 7 Cathedral Avenue, Mbale – Uganda. Founded in 2022, it is fundamentally free from political, clans, religion and ethnic divisions. Its missions are to protect, repair, motivate, educate, care for and help build people in difficulty. We have worked continuously with the communities through projects of education, rehabilitation and skilling of youths and teen mothers through our campaign 'LETS SAVE THE FUTURE GENERATION' Under this campaign, we have seen major transformations in many who once used their bodies to fend for themselves and their families give it all up , acquiring skills , employed and are living a much better life . We have also taken many back to school.

WE BELIEVE WHERE HOPE IS REBORN, THERE IS A REASON TO LIVE, WHICH IS PURPOSE, AND IT CAN ONLY BE FOUND IN GOD.
